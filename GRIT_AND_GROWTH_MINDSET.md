# Grit and Growth Mindset

## Question 1 - Grit

A person can achieve big goals by working hard consistently and not quitting when problems come. 
People with grit stay focused and continue learning even during difficult times.

## Question 2 - Introduction to Growth Mindset

The video explains that skills and intelligence can improve with learning and practice. 
Instead of being afraid of mistakes, people should use failures as a chance to learn and grow.

## Question 3 - Internal Locus of Control

Internal Locus of Control means believing that our actions affect our future and success. 
The main point of the video is that we should focus on improving ourselves instead of blaming situations or other people.

## Question 4 - Key Points to Build Growth Mindset

- Learn from mistakes
- Accept challenges
- Practice regularly
- Stay positive
- Take feedback seriously
- Keep improving step by step
- Be patient while learning
- Never stop learning new things

## Question 5 - My Ideas to Build Growth Mindset

I will practice coding every day and try to improve slowly. 
I will learn from my mistakes instead of feeling discouraged. 
I will ask questions whenever I do not understand something and take feedback from mentors seriously. 
I will also build projects regularly and keep learning new technologies to improve my skills.
