# Listening and Active Communication

## Introduction
Effective communication is one of the most important skills in both personal and professional life.This document covers Active Listening and different types of communication styles.


## 1. Active Listening

### Question 1: What are the steps/strategies to do Active Listening?

 * Give full attention

 * Maintain eye contact

 * Avoid interrupting

 * Ask questions

### Question 2: According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening?

 * Listen carefully

 * Understand the message 

 * Give feedback 


## 3. Reflection

### Question 3: What are the obstacles in your listening process?

- Getting distracted by thoughts  
- Thinking about my response before the other person finishes    

### Question 4: What can you do to improve your listening?

To improve my listening:
- Avoid distractions  
- Take notes when necessary  
- Ask clarifying questions   


## 4. Types of Communication

### 1. Passive Communication
- Avoids expressing thoughts or feelings   

### 2. Aggressive Communication 
- Can lead to conflict 

### 4. Assertive Communication 
- Clear and respectful expression of thoughts  
- Builds healthy relationships  

## Question 5: When do you switch to Passive communication style?

I tend to become passive when:
- I want to avoid conflict  

## Question 6: When do you switch into Aggressive communication styles?

I may become aggressive :
- When i am stressed or frustrated  

## Question 7: When do you switch into Passive-Aggressive communication styles?

I may become passive-aggressive when:
- I am upset but don’t express it directly   

## Question 8: How can you make your communication assertive?

To improve assertive communication:
* Speak clearly and confidently 
* Listen actively before responding
